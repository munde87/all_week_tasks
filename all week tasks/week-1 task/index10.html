My Trip to Surabardi, Nagpur

Date: 8 August 2025

How to Reach

Surabardi is located near Nagpur and can be reached by road via local buses, private taxis, or personal vehicles.

Best Time to Visit

October to February is the best season to enjoy the greenery and peaceful surroundings of Surabardi.

My Experience

I enjoyed the serene lake, the calm atmosphere, and spending quality time amidst nature at Surabardi.
